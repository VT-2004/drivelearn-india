## Project Overview

Scope Summary

DriveLearn India is a web-based SaaS marketplace platform that digitizes the driving school industry in India. The platform connects three key groups — driving school owners, instructors, and learners — through a single system, while a Super Admin (B2World's internal team) oversees and manages the entire platform.

Driving school owners can register their business on the platform, get verified by the Super Admin, and then manage their branches, instructors, courses, and pricing. Learners can search for driving schools by location, compare pricing and ratings, book courses, and pay securely online. Instructors track attendance and lesson progress for the students assigned to them.

The platform generates revenue through subscriptions — driving schools pay a recurring fee to remain listed and active on the platform — making this a two-sided marketplace, similar in structure to platforms like Zomato or Practo, but for the driving school industry.

The system is being built using React.js on the frontend and Node.js/Express with PostgreSQL on the backend, with JWT-based authentication, Razorpay for payments, AWS S3 for file storage, and Google Maps for location-based search. The final deliverable will include a public marketing website, four role-based dashboards (Super Admin, School Owner, Instructor, Learner), and a full booking, payment, and subscription workflow, built with scalability and future nationwide expansion in mind.
